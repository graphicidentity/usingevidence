    <p class="lead">Fred Carden is an evaluation specialist with <strong>30+ years</strong> of experience in development research evaluation and methodology. His work emphasizes <em>how evidence is used</em> — from policy influence to organization‑wide learning.</p>

    <h2 class="h3">Highlights</h2>
    <ul class="list-group list-group-flush">
      <li class="list-group-item">Founded <strong>Using Evidence Inc.</strong> (2015) to improve the <strong>use of research and evaluative evidence</strong> in decision making.</li>
      <li class="list-group-item">Leadership & advisory: Board — Partnership for Economic Policy; Advisor — Global Development Network, Doing Research.</li>
      <li class="list-group-item">Editorial boards: <em>American Journal of Evaluation</em>, <em>Journal of Evidence & Policy</em>, and <em>New Directions for Evaluation</em>.</li>
      <li class="list-group-item">Education: <strong>PhD</strong> (Université de Montréal, 1990); <strong>Fellowship in Sustainability Science</strong> (Harvard, 2008).</li>
      <li class="list-group-item">Based in <strong>Ottawa, Canada</strong>.</li>
    </ul>
